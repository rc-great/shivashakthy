Her Spiritual Journey

Initially, my spiritual practice was limited to traditional rituals, prayers, and vows. This changed dramatically when I developed a profound aspiration for self-awareness (atma chaitanyam) and self-knowledge (atma jnanam). This pivot marked the true commencement of my deep spiritual journey. Over several years, through dedicated pursuit of this knowledge and rigorous spiritual discipline (tapasya), I was granted the path of Shiva Shakti Kundalini Sadhana. In this profound spiritual journey, I engaged deeply in meditation, pranayama, and energetic practices, which led to a profound internal transformation. The practice of navigating the Sushumna nadi was effortless, and I attained complete knowledge. Nagamaninanda Bharathi, a person who has mastered Kundalini Sadhana and has a complete understanding of all these matters, knows the path to Samadhi and what it entails. She understands what Samadhi is, what it yields, and how to attain it.

Today, she is dedicated to guiding seekers on their own spiritual path through the sacred science of Kundalini awakening. With wisdom rooted in tradition and experience, she helps individuals connect with their inner divine nature, cultivating balance, clarity, and spiritual growth.

Through Shiva Shakthi Kundalini Sadhana, she shares time-tested techniques for:

  • Awakening dormant spiritual energies
  • Attaining inner peace and harmony
  • Deepening meditation and pranayama practices
  • Experiencing higher states of consciousness

Her mission is to empower every soul to walk the path of self-realization and awaken the limitless potential within.
